Parsers
=======

``Parser`` objects take the input to the model and convert it to numbers that the model can
consume. We have provided a base class ``BaseParser`` on top of which you can build your own
parser. We have also provided a few basic parsers such as ``ImageParser`` and ``TextParser``.
